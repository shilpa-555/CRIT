Chiranjeevi Reddy Institute of Engineering and Technology (CRIT), Anantapur was established in 2008 with a noble objective of promoting technical education in the backward region of Anantapur.

Chiranjeevi Reddy Institute of Engineering & Technology will emerge as a leading institution that creates and disseminates knowledge.

Its objective is to produce highly innovative, motivated global leaders in the field of Engineering education. The institute is affiliated to JNTU and approved by AICTE, Government of India.
